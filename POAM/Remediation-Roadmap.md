# 1. Purpose

The purpose of this remediation roadmap is to define a structured plan for addressing cybersecurity risks, vulnerabilities, compliance gaps, and security weaknesses identified during the Able Behavioral Health security assessment.

This roadmap supports:

- Risk reduction
- HIPAA compliance
- Security maturity improvement
- Continuous monitoring
- Audit readiness
- Operational resilience

---

# 2. Remediation Strategy

The organization will prioritize remediation activities based on:

- Risk severity
- Business impact
- Regulatory requirements
- Threat likelihood
- Operational dependencies

Remediation efforts are divided into:

- Immediate Actions (0–30 Days)
- Short-Term Improvements (30–90 Days)
- Mid-Term Improvements (90–180 Days)
- Long-Term Strategic Enhancements (180+ Days)

---

# 3. Current Security Priorities

The following areas represent the highest organizational security priorities:

| Priority Area | Risk Level |
|---|---|
| Phishing Protection | Critical |
| Ransomware Defense | Critical |
| Endpoint Security | High |
| Patch Management | High |
| Access Control | High |
| Vendor Risk Management | Moderate |
| Cloud Security Governance | Moderate |

---

# 4. Immediate Actions (0–30 Days)

## 4.1 Expand Phishing Defenses

### Objective

Reduce risk of credential theft and business email compromise.

### Planned Actions

- Conduct phishing awareness campaign
- Deploy phishing simulation exercises
- Improve email filtering configurations
- Review suspicious login alerts

### Responsible Team

- IT Security
- Compliance Department

### Expected Outcome

Reduced phishing susceptibility and improved employee awareness.

---

## 4.2 Accelerate Critical Patch Management

### Objective

Reduce exposure to known vulnerabilities.

### Planned Actions

- Identify overdue patches
- Prioritize critical system remediation
- Reduce patch deployment timelines
- Validate successful deployments

### Responsible Team

- IT Operations

### Expected Outcome

Reduced vulnerability exposure across endpoints and servers.

---

## 4.3 Expand Endpoint Protection Coverage

### Objective

Improve ransomware detection and prevention capabilities.

### Planned Actions

- Complete Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR) deployment
- Validate antivirus functionality
- Enable centralized alerting
- Review endpoint policy configurations

### Responsible Team

- IT Security

### Expected Outcome

Improved endpoint visibility and malware response capability.

---

# 5. Short-Term Improvements (30–90 Days)

## 5.1 Strengthen Access Management

### Objective

Improve least privilege enforcement and reduce unauthorized access risks.

### Planned Actions

- Conduct privileged access reviews
- Review inactive accounts
- Improve role-based access controls
- Expand logging for administrative accounts

### Responsible Team

- Compliance Department
- IT Administration

### Expected Outcome

Improved access governance and reduced insider threat exposure.

---

## 5.2 Backup Validation and Recovery Testing

### Objective

Improve operational resilience during cybersecurity incidents.

### Planned Actions

- Conduct backup restoration testing
- Validate recovery time objectives
- Review backup retention policies
- Document recovery procedures

### Responsible Team

- IT Operations

### Expected Outcome

Improved disaster recovery readiness.

---

## 5.3 Cloud Security Review

### Objective

Reduce risks associated with cloud misconfigurations.

### Planned Actions

- Review Microsoft 365 sharing permissions
- Restrict public sharing capabilities
- Review conditional access policies
- Validate MFA enforcement

### Responsible Team

- Cloud Administration Team

### Expected Outcome

Improved cloud security posture and reduced data exposure risks.

---
